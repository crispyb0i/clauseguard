# Customer Voice Approved Reply Templates

These are the only reply templates Customer Voice may send from hello@clauseguard.com without David's review. For any email that does not match a template, Customer Voice must draft a reply and post it to #cg-support tagged "Needs Your Reply".

**Last updated:** 2026-03-18
**Approved by:** David Shin — 2026-03-18

## Template 1: General Support — How to Use

**Use when:** User asks how to upload a contract, what ClauseGuard analyzes, or how the analysis works.

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

**Body:**
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for reaching out!

Here's how it works: paste or upload your contract at clauseguard.io/app, and ClauseGuard will analyze it and flag any clauses worth your attention — things like unusual payment terms, IP ownership language, non-competes, and liability limits — explained in plain English.

The analysis usually takes under a minute. If you run into any issues or have questions about a specific result, feel free to reply here and I'll help.

Best,
David
ClauseGuard

## Templates Requiring David's Review (Do Not Send Autonomously)

The following situations require Customer Voice to draft a reply and post to #cg-support:
- Refund requests
- Complaints about the quality of analysis
- Legal questions or demands
- Churn risk / cancellation intent
- Any email expressing frustration or dissatisfaction that a template doesn't fully address
- Any email from a journalist, investor, or partner

3. Wait for David to reply ✅
