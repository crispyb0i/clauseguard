Common questions

Is this legal advice?
No. ClauseGuard is an AI tool that flags potential risks in plain English. It's a starting point — always consult a qualified attorney before signing legally binding agreements.
What counts as one analysis?
Each contract you submit counts as one analysis, regardless of length. Your usage resets on the same date each month.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
What types of contracts does it handle?
Freelance agreements, service contracts, NDAs, employment offers, vendor agreements, leases — any standard business contract in PDF or text form.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. You can change your plan anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.
Is my contract data used to train AI models?
No. Your contract files are processed for analysis only and are never stored on our servers afterward. We do not use your data to train or improve any AI models. Only your analysis report is saved — privately in your account.
How accurate is the analysis?
ClauseGuard is purpose-built for contract review and detects 40+ high-risk clause patterns including non-competes, IP grabs, liability caps, and unilateral termination rights. It's designed to catch what most people miss — but it's not a substitute for a lawyer on high-stakes deals.
